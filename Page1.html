City of Nottingham

The award-winning Nottingham Tourism Centre is centrally located on Smithy Row, just off the Old Market Square. The friendly team have a wealth of information Nottingham and are always happy to help visitors which advice on events and attractions, accommodation and transport around the county. They have a range of free maps and guides to help you find your way around the city, as well as a wealth of brochures and leaflets showing what's on and things to do in Nottingham. You can also book tickets for a variety of Nottinghamshire attractions, including the National Justice Museum and City of Caves, concerts at St Mary's Church and tours with the multi award-winning Ezekial Bone. They recently expanded their range of great gifts and souvenirs, adding over 250 new gift ranges that are either made locally, inspired by Nottingham, or are from local independent businesses. Take your pick from locally produced preserves and luxury, loose leaf teas, amazing upcycled art, and, of course, a range of Robin Hood inspired items...
